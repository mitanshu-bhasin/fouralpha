The Distinction: Champions vs. Master Black Belts (MBBs)

The Four Alpha Champion (The Visionary Sponsor): Champions are non-technical leaders—Prime Ministers, UN Secretary-Generals, CEOs of multinational conglomerates. They do not calculate the Stochastic Differential Equations (SDEs). Their role is to set the planetary vision, allocate capital, remove political roadblocks, and demand that all global policies adhere strictly to the Dwivedi-Nash Cooperative Equilibrium.

The Master Black Belt (The Apex Architect): The MBB is the supreme technical authority. They act as the mathematical conscience of the Champions. They design cross-border Macro-RCTs, audit international treaties, and train the global army of Four Alpha Black Belts.
