THE FOUR ALPHA MODEL

Quantifying the actual trajectory of human evolution.

Space-Time Observation

"Deepak, I have observed your species across millennia. I see empires rise and collapse... immense behavioral friction. If I am to understand the underlying mathematics of the human operating system, tell me: what is the actual trajectory of your evolution?"

Baseline Response

"Humanity has always been driven by four fundamental engines... Whether you observe a primitive tribe 10,000 years ago or an AI-driven nation today, every action is an attempt to optimize one of four invariant variables."

Beneath the noise of history, humanity has always been driven by four fundamental engines. Ancient sages in Sanatan Dharma called them Purusharthas, forming the basis of our modern Purushartha Process Excellence. To strip away cultural localized language, we map them as the Four Alpha Domains (α1, α2, α3, α4).
